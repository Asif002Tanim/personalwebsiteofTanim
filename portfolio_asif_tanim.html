About

An engineer who
bridges analysis & impact

I'm an Industrial & Production Engineering student at BUET, currently in Level 4, with a passion for converting complex operational challenges into financially meaningful solutions. My work bridges data analysis, process engineering, and leadership.

My industrial attachment at British American Tobacco Bangladesh gave me hands-on exposure to real manufacturing challenges — I led a project that generated ~7 lakh BDT in annual savings by redesigning a drum-draining technique that addressed 18–19 lakh BDT in annual material loss.

Beyond engineering, I serve as Cultural Secretary at AIPE BUET, author research papers, and have competed nationally in chess, physics, and mathematics olympiads. I thrive at the intersection of technical precision and creative leadership.
